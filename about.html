cloudspotting.app beta

Aussie weather radar simplified!

cloudspotting.app is a web app that shows every Australian Bureau of Meteorology radar image animated over your exact location.

I was in the Blue Mountains one day about to climb up a cliff with a scary stormcloud on the horizon, but I couldn't tell where I was on the BOM radar image - so I built this.

This website uses data under copyright of the Austraian Government Bureau of Meteorology. I make every effort to show current data, and I cache requests to go easy on their servers.

Feel free to drop me a message at jake@jakecoppinger.com with any suggestions or feedback - I promise I'll reply!

The codebase is open source at github.com/jakecoppinger/cloudspotting.app and PRs are very welcome!
